75 * into those specific section.
76 * e.g
77 * - CFG_TUSB_MEM SECTION : __attribute__ (( section(".usb_ram") ))
78 * - CFG_TUSB_MEM_ALIGN : __attribute__ ((aligned(4)))
79 */
